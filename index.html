The Gleam™ Photo Contest: Open Now!

Contest To Win The Gleam Photo Contest! $25,000 Grand Prize Up for Grabs

Gleam Photography Presents: Presenting the Gleam™ Photo Contest: a kaleidoscope of cultures captured through the lens! Delve into the vibrant hues of global diversity as participants showcase the richness of various traditions and customs. From bustling cityscapes to serene landscapes, every snapshot tells a unique story of cultural fusion and exploration.

Click Here to Vote

How to Vote

Starting February 1, 2024:

STEP 1: Access our Voting Platform

  • Look for options to vote using your social media account.

STEP 2: Vote your favorite chefs* using either Instagram, Facebook or Email.

  • Once logged in, navigate to the voting section or page. Explore the chef profiles and dishes showcased in the contest.

Vote Now

* Posting as a story on Instagram or Facebook is not permitted for contest entry.

Nominee Selection

Our expert panel of judges at Gleam will carefully select the top three nominees in each category, meticulously evaluating based on four criteria: originality (25%), visual impact (25%), creativity (25%), and technical execution (25%). The final winners will be determined through a public vote, ensuring transparency and community engagement.

Prizes

If your photograph is selected as one of the top entries, you have the chance to win.

  • One (1) $25,000 Grand Prize winner selected across all categories.
  • Three (3) $5,000 Runner-up Prizes for different photography categories.
